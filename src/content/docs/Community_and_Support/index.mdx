# Community and Support

Welcome to the Fleek Community and Support page! At Fleek, we believe in fostering an open, trustless, and collaborative community that empowers developers and users to build on a decentralized web. Whether you're a seasoned builder or new to the internet, this is your hub for engaging with our community, learning from resources, and contributing to our mission.

<div className="my-12 bg-gray-dark-6 p-[1px]" />

## Our Open Source and Trustless Ethos

Fleek is built on the principles of transparency, trustlessness, and decentralization. We aim to:

1. Empower Builders: Provide developers with tools to create scalable, secure, and decentralized applications.

2. Encourage Collaboration: Maintain an open-source ecosystem where anyone can contribute, improve, and innovate.

3. Promote Trustlessness: Ensure our platform aligns with decentralized principles by minimizing reliance on centralized entities.

Join us in creating a onchain future that is open, inclusive, and accessible to all.
