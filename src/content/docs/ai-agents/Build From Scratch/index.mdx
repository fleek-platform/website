# Building an AI agent from scratch

If you don’t have a pre-configured characterfile, you can create your AI agent from scratch by filling in the required details.

Follow these steps:

Click on "Build from scratch" on the "Get started" screen.

![](./b-flow-4.png)

Fill in your agent details, and you’ll be prompted to provide essential information for your agent.
![](./b-future-flow-5.png)
